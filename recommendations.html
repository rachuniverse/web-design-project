Jenni Raincloud is a blog written by Jennifer Phillips. Her blog describes multiple DIY skincare products that use natural materials.

The Natural Beauty Workshop

The Natural Beauty Workshop is a blog written by mutiple different authors, but the primary author and editor is Mary Helen Leonard. The recipe formulator and community moderator is Kelly Owens. Organic Beauty Blogger

The Organic Beauty Blogger is a blog written by Inga. This blog consists of posts about natural skincare, makeup, haircare, bodycare, and lifestyle. Her blog focuses on products related to green beauty.

The Green Product Junkie is a blog written by Katie. Her blog covers the topics of natural & organic skincare, makeup, bodycare, haircare, and ecohome. Majority of the posts on her blog are about green/organic and natural products.

Small Bits of Loveliness is a blog written by Jana. Her blog goes over a multitude of natural skincare, beauty, and lifestyle posts. The main theme of her blog is green beauty. Jana's blog also goes over products that are natural, organic, and cruelty free.
