About Us

Who We Are

The Glow Guru originated from a need for community-based, science-backed skincare. In 2021, The Glow Guru launched. We sourced an amazing team of writers and experts. Since our launch, our community has grown tenfold. We have been mentioned in various publications, such as Glamour, Allure, and Elle. Our founding values are reliability, openness, and honesty. We continue to uphold these as we expand. Our articles are written by skincare lovers for skincare lovers!

Our Mission

Here at The Glow Guru, we are dedicated to making skincare approachable and personalized. We only promote real, tried and tested products. Skincare experts curate and write for us to provide you the most up to date information on skincare research and formulas. Everyone's skin is different, and that's why we heavily focus on personalization. We don't believe in one size fits all solutions. Finally, in providing this useful resource, we are creating a community of skincare enthusiasts.
